Due Diligence Mastery

FAQ'S

Frequently Asked Questions.

What does Positive Hills Consulting do?

We are a business consulting firm dedicated to helping African startups, SMEs, and visionary founders build structured, investor-ready, and scalable businesses. Our services include strategy development, investor matchmaking, talent development, and digital transformation.

Do you only work with businesses in Nigeria?

No. While we are based in Nigeria, we work with entrepreneurs and investors across Africa and the diaspora. Our consulting, strategy sessions, and digital training programs are accessible globally.

How do you help businesses become investor-ready?

We help you build the structure investors want to see — from refining your business model and developing solid financials to embedding governance and preparing pitch decks. We also facilitate direct connections with purpose-aligned investors.

What makes Positive Hills different from other consulting firms?

We go beyond surface-level advice. Our approach is data-driven, impact-focused, and hands-on. We combine strategy, capital access, and talent development, helping you grow sustainably and ethically, not just fast.

How can I start working with you?

You can reach out via our contact form, email, or social media platforms. We’ll schedule a discovery session to understand your goals and recommend the right support path for your business.

Can investors partner with you?

Absolutely. We collaborate with ethical, impact-driven investors looking for well-structured, scalable ventures. Our ecosystem ensures that investors are matched with ventures that align with their values and growth expectations.
